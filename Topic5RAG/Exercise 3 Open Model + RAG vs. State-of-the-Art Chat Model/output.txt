How do I fix a slipping transmission band?
A slipping transmission band in a Model T is usually corrected by tightening the adjusting screws located on the transmission cover. Remove the inspection plate, loosen the lock nut, turn the adjusting screw slightly to tighten the band, then retighten the lock nut. Adjust gradually and test to avoid over-tightening, which can cause dragging.

What oil should I use in a Model T engine?
Model T engines traditionally use a **non-detergent motor oil**, commonly SAE 30 in warm weather and lighter grades such as SAE 20 in colder conditions. Because the Model T lacks a modern oil filtration system, non-detergent oil is preferred to prevent debris circulation.
